Who we are

Gsudz Detailing provides auto detailing services in Dayton, Tennessee and surrounding areas. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at gavdetails01@gmail.com or (423) 394-1698.

Information we collect

  • Contact details: name, phone number, email (if provided).
  • Service details: vehicle type, service requested, location/area, preferred day/time, and any notes you submit.

How we use your information

  • To respond to quote requests and schedule appointments.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier. You can opt out of non-essential texts at any time by replying STOP.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to provide services (for example, website form delivery providers), comply with law, or protect our rights.

We keep customer communications and service records for as long as reasonably necessary for scheduling, warranty/issue handling, accounting, and legitimate business purposes.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We use reasonable safeguards, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.
